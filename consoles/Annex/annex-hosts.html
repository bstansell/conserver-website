This page contains additional information for my Micro-Annex Console Guide, and is specific to the Xylogics micro-Annex, the Bay Networks 2000, and the Nortel Networks 2000 console server products. Information on other brands of console servers, as well as links to logging software and basic serial information can be found on my Greater Scroll of Console Knowledge.

If you connect your SUN computers to a Console Server, you should take a quick read through our serial BREAK testing pages for information you may find useful.

This document is meant to show which adapters you would use to connect a variety of devices to micro-Annex console server ports. The list presumes the following:

  1. The adapters are purchased from APA Cables & Networks.
  2. The adapters are connected with an 8-wire straight-through cable, not a 'rolled' cable.

APA Cables & Networks sells all of the adapters listed, as well as the specialty cables. See the APA Cables & Networks Annex Kit page for part numbers and other info.)

The table below shows various devices, followed by;

  • port is indicates what signalling in coming from that device.
  • Console indicates what cable or adapter you would use to connect that device to a micro-Annex RJ-45 Console Port

More information is available in the Annex Console Guide.
